Transport your taste buds to the vibrant flavors of Indonesia with Nasi Goreng, a beloved dish that will tantalize your senses

Ingredients:

3 cups of cooked rice (preferably cold)
2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
3 cloves of garlic, minced
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 eggs, lightly beaten
200 grams of diced chicken, shrimp, or your choice of protein
2 tablespoons of soy sauce
1 tablespoon of kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
Salt and pepper to taste
2 spring onions, sliced
1 carrot, finely chopped
1/2 cup of peas (fresh or frozen)
Lime wedges for garnish
Fried eggs (optional) for topping

Instructions:

1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add minced garlic and chopped onions. Saut√© until fragrant and onions turn translucent.
3. Push the garlic and onions to one side of the pan and pour the beaten eggs onto the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
4. Add the diced chicken, shrimp, or protein of your choice to the pan. Cook until the protein is fully cooked through.
5. Add the cold cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for a few minutes until the rice is heated through.
6. Pour in the soy sauce and kecap manis (sweet soy sauce). Mix well with the rice and protein.
7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning according to your preference.
8. Add the sliced spring onions, chopped carrots, and peas to the pan. Stir-fry for a few more minutes until the vegetables are tender.
9. Remove from heat and garnish with lime wedges.
10. For an extra touch, top each serving with a fried egg.
11. Serve hot and enjoy the aromatic and flavorful Nasi Goreng!

Note: Feel free to customize the recipe by adding additional vegetables, such as bell peppers or bean sprouts, and adjusting the seasonings to suit your taste preferences.
